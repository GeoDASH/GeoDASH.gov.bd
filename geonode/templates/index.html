Import your own layers

Layers are the contents of a map. To get started adding layers, sign in to the site, click on upload your layers/data, choose the data format for Vector (Example: .shp, .shx, .dbf, .prj) or Raster (.tiff, .img). Make sure your data projection is WGS 84 or BTM.

Make pretty maps with GeoDASH

To create a new map go to your contributed or existing layers and click the “create map” link.This will take you to the “Map Composer” with a base layer loaded.To add data layers from GeoDASH click on the plus button located upper left side of the screen.This will open a dialog listing all the layers available on the GeoDASH.Select the layers you wish to add, then hit the Add Layers button.
